About The Founder close

Brian P. Enright, Ph.D.

Business Acumen
Over his career, Brian has coached and consulted with leaders at all levels including C-level executives of organizations ranging from Fortune 500 to mid-sized family owned businesses. He has experience working with organizations from a variety of industries including healthcare, finance, manufacturing, engineering, construction, recycling, retail, government, not-for-profit, law enforcement and U.S. military operations.

Expertise
Brian has more than ten years of experience partnering with leaders of publicly traded, privately owned, not-for-profit and government agencies to help build capacity and morale within the most important resource in any organization – its people. His expertise includes psychological assessment for selection and development, identifying the traits needed to succeed in specific roles, leadership development, executive coaching, team building, conflict resolution and generating creative talent-management solutions to fit specific client needs.

Leadership
While he served as a Captain in the United States Air Force, Brian honed his leadership skills and consulted with high-ranking military officials regarding leadership effectiveness and staffing issues for high-impact and sensitive positions. In recognition of his service, he was selected as Officer of the Year in his Squadron and he was awarded the Air Force Commendation Medal.

Education and Research
Brian graduated with highest honors from Butler University and later earned his M.A. and Ph.D. in psychology from the University of Missouri - St. Louis. His doctoral dissertation focused on psychological assessment in employee selection. His research has been published in The Journal of Clinical Psychology and presented at various professional conferences. He has conducted seminars and workshops on numerous topics including professional ethics, leadership development, culture change, stress management, and team building.  He has taught graduate level courses in the practical application of cognitive and personality assessment.

Affiliations and Interests
Brian is a Licensed Psychologist. He is a member of the American Psychological Association, Society for Industrial and Organizational Psychology, and Sigma Xi Research Society. For the past few years, he has traveled extensively and has worked in several countries throughout Europe. He enjoys volunteering in areas that support community building and that promote children's interest in science.
